Pink Valvet Cupcake($9.50)

Pink velvet cupcakes start with soft, tender buttermilk cake. It’s topped with a tangy cream cheese frosting, giving you a delicious pink velvet cupcake, perfect for your Valentine! For these pink velvet cupcakes, we used moist and fluffy buttermilk cupcake recipe, then topped them with a traditional cream cheese frosting. We used 2-3 drops of pink food coloring to get this pretty color. The cupcake is really the best part of our bakry, it’s so soft and melts in your mouth. The tangy cream cheese frosting breaks down the sweetness a little bit and it’s a perfect combination. We think this will be perfect for a girl’s birthday party as well as your Valentine’s day celebrations.
